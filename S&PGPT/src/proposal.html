S&PGPT: The Idea

Project Proposal

AI-Powered Tool for Analyzing Financial Statements

Objective: The goal of this project is to develop an innovative AI system using OpenAI's ChatGPT that can answer questions regarding the Income Statements (I/S) and Balance Sheets (B/S) of S&P 500 companies by analyzing their 10-K filings. This tool aims to enhance accounting education at the University of Washington by making the learning process more interactive and engaging.

Background: Understanding financial statements is a critical component of the accounting curriculum, providing students with essential knowledge and skills for their future careers. By leveraging AI technology, this project seeks to create a dynamic learning environment that improves students' comprehension and retention of financial statement analysis.

Project Plan:

  • Data Collection and Analysis: Conduct an in-depth analysis of 10-K filings from S&P 500 companies to extract relevant information regarding their I/S and B/S.
  • Model Training: Train OpenAI's ChatGPT on the collected data to ensure it can accurately interpret and respond to questions about financial statements.
  • Interface Development: Develop a user-friendly conversational interface that allows students to interact with the trained AI model.
  • Testing and Fine-Tuning: Conduct extensive testing of the system using real-world questions about S&P 500 Income Statements and Balance Sheets. Fine-tune the AI model to enhance its accuracy and reliability.

Expected Outcomes: By implementing this AI-powered tool, students will gain a more engaging and practical understanding of financial statements. The project is expected to:

  • Enhance the interactive learning experience in accounting and finance courses.
  • Provide a valuable educational resource that can be utilized in classrooms nationwide.
  • Contribute to the advancement of AI technology in the field of accounting and finance education.

Collaboration and Support: The success of this project hinges on collaboration and support from experienced professionals in the field. Expertise in accounting and AI development will be crucial in ensuring the project's effectiveness and accuracy.

Conclusion: This project presents an exciting opportunity to revolutionize accounting and financial education at the University of Washington. By integrating AI technology into the learning process, we can provide students with a unique and valuable educational experience that prepares them for their future careers.
